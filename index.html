Projects

Here’s one example of how I approach analysis work. I’m continuing to build this section as I add more projects over time.

NBA Fantasy Decision Model

November 2025

This was a team class project where we analyzed more than 82,000 NBA player records to support fantasy basketball decisions. My role was leading the exploratory data analysis, which meant digging into the data, identifying meaningful patterns, and helping shape the modeling direction based on what the data was actually showing.

One of the biggest takeaways from my work was recognizing both linear and non-linear relationships in the dataset. That insight helped guide the team toward using Linear Regression and Random Forest models rather than relying on a one-model approach. The final output turned the analysis into practical Start, Bench, and Cut recommendations for fantasy users.

  • Role: Led EDA and helped determine model selection strategy
  • Tools: Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Excel, Git/GitHub
  • Outcome: Produced user-facing recommendations based on model-backed player evaluation
